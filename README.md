# Redefine Innovations

Redefine Innovations is a technology and digital transformation company specializing in AI-powered ecommerce, Microsoft Dynamics 365, Product Information Management (PIM), Order Management Systems (OMS), and intelligent business automation.

Our flagship platform, Redefine Suite, helps B2B, DTC, retail, wholesale, and distribution businesses streamline product information, optimize order operations, connect enterprise systems, and scale digital commerce through intelligent automation.
